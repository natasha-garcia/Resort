The Accommodations at Atlantic Trails

What is a bungalow?
Our luxury bungalows are permanent structure four feet off the ground. Each bungalow has canvas walls, a wooden flor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the bungalows furnished?
Each bungalow is furnished with a queen-sized bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxury camping expereince also includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
